Chaleur


Melancholy

2017

Medium: Mixed Media (Acrylic paint, dyes, and other oil-based paint) on canvas.

18in x 24in.

Strong and unique artwork rich in textures, colors and shapes, with a burst of life and fresh visual identity.


Blossom Hills

2019

Medium: Acrylic on canvas

3ft x 5ft.

Welcome to a fantasy Fall landscape. This painting is bold and highly textured. I used brushes to blend the background, with palette knife strokes to create crisp bright contrasts and movement. The painting has metallic highlights to catch the light.


Wave

2018

Medium: Murano Glass

12in x 8in.

My aesthetic embraces irregularity, experimentation, and a wide range of color combinations. The traditional Venetian cane methods and incalmo techniques can be used in new ways to create varied line widths, broken canes, and distorted patterns.


Warm Colors Abstract

2016

Medium: Photograph

4in x 6in.

Warm colors exude a feeling of warmth and energy. They are used to denote alarm, motion, light, heat, and to portray anger, fear, love, and desire.
