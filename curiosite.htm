Curiosite


Curiosity

2012

Medium: Acrylic paint on canvas.

4ft x 6ft.

This piece is of the tiger gazing upon a butterfly. Showing the strength of the tiger as child-like and bewildered. Expressing purity, spirituality and simplicity to present compositions that develop through the intuitive process.


Curiosity and Condemnation

2010

Medium: Acrylic paint on canvas

18in x 24in.

The gaze of curiosity and condemnation. With bold brush strokes and geometric shapes, this extracts order from chaos to create this characteristic, emotionally charged painting.


Curiosity

2016

Medium: Bronze Metal

26in x 62in.

Abstract figure in dynamic pose perched on bent steel stand with warm color patina.


Cat and Butterfly

2011

Medium: Aluminum

15in x 7in.

Who’s more curious, the cat or the butterfly? Sand-cast, powdercoated aluminum has the look of bronze without the weight. Hand-painted green and gold finish.
