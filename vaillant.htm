Vaillant


Boats

2020

Medium: Watercolor on canvas.

18in x 3ft.

This piece represents the valliant effort of the seamen against the unforgiving sea. Using vivid colors and sharp black lines, you can almost feel the current of the ocean whisk you away.


Prince

2012

Medium: Charcoal on canvas

12in x 18in.

Deep hues of black and orange make the Valiant Prince pop but also fade into the page, allowing for a feeling of the seriousness of war, but the softness of peace.


Woman

2013

Medium: Bronze Metal

18in x 18.5in.

A spectacular bronze sculpture entitled Woman. Bronze is mounted on a thick piece of walnut with beautiful graining.


Continuity

2014

Medium: Bronze Metal

46in x 35in.

This work aims to break down blocks of movement and convert them into curves that extended past the shape of a human body, before reassembling them as a forward-marching figure.
